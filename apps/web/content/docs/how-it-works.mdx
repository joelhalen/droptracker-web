# How it works

When you receive a drop in-game, here's what happens:

1. **The RuneLite plugin** detects the drop and submits it to DropTracker.
2. **The intake pipeline** values the item, records it against your account, and
   updates the relevant leaderboards.
3. **Notifications** are posted to your clan's configured Discord channels
   (subject to that group's [settings](/docs/group-settings)).
4. **The website** reflects the change in real time — leaderboards and profiles
   update live without a refresh.

## Identity

DropTracker is Discord-native. Your **Discord account** is your identity on the
site, and each **OSRS account** you play is linked to it once you
[claim it](/docs/link-account). A single Discord user can own several OSRS
accounts.

## Groups

A **group** is a clan, tied to a Wise Old Man group and a Discord server.
Membership drives the clan leaderboards and determines who notifications are sent
for. See [creating a group](/docs/create-group).
