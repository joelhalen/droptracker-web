# Frequently asked questions

## My drop didn't show up — why?

Most often the value was below your group's **minimum value to notify**, or the
group requires a screenshot. Check the group's
[settings](/docs/group-settings). Your personal totals still update even when a
notification is suppressed.

## Can I track more than one account?

Yes. Link as many OSRS accounts to your Discord as you like — each is
[claimed](/docs/link-account) separately, and all of them count toward your
groups.

## How are leaderboards calculated?

Loot is aggregated into per-period leaderboards (all-time, monthly, weekly, and
daily) and served in real time. Hidden players are excluded.

## How do I leave or hide from a group?

Group admins can hide a player from the **Members** tab. To control your own
visibility everywhere, use your [privacy settings](/docs/privacy-and-notifications).

## Is my data private?

You control visibility from [Settings](/settings) — you can keep your profile
private or hide entirely from leaderboards.
