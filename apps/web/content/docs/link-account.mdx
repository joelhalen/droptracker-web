# Linking your OSRS account

OSRS accounts are created automatically the first time the plugin submits a drop,
keyed on the account's hash. To show that account on your profile, you **claim**
it from your dashboard.

## Steps

1. [Sign in with Discord](/api/auth/login).
2. Go to [My accounts](/dashboard).
3. Choose **claim an account** and follow the plugin-assisted verification: the
   site issues a short code that you enter in the RuneLite plugin (or vice
   versa). This proves you own the account.

> Claims are always verified through the plugin — an account is never linked on
> name alone. This keeps leaderboards honest.

Once claimed, the account's loot, ranks, and submissions appear on your
[profile](/dashboard) and count toward any groups you belong to.
