# Getting started

DropTracker records Old School RuneScape drops, collection-log slots, personal
bests, combat achievements, and pets — then ranks players and clans on
real-time leaderboards.

There are two ways loot gets in:

1. **The RuneLite plugin** (recommended) automatically submits your drops as you
   play.
2. **Manual submission** on the website for one-off entries with optional
   screenshot/video proof.

## In three steps

1. **Install the RuneLite plugin** and log into the game — your account is
   created automatically the first time you submit.
2. **Sign in with Discord** on this site, then
   [claim your OSRS account](/docs/link-account) so your drops show on your
   profile.
3. **Join or create a group** to compete on clan leaderboards and unlock
   notifications.

Once you're set up, head to the [leaderboards](/leaderboards) to see where you
rank.
