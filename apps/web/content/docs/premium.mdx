# Premium subscriptions

Groups can subscribe to a recurring **premium** tier that unlocks extras like
seasonal lootboards, extended history, custom board themes, and priority
processing. Compare plans on the [premium page](/premium).

## Managing a subscription

From the group's **Subscription** tab (`/groups/{id}/subscription`), an owner or
admin can:

- **Subscribe** to a tier or **switch** between tiers.
- **Cancel** — the group keeps its benefits until the end of the paid period.
- **Resume** a subscription that's set to cancel.
- **Manage billing** — open the payment provider's portal to update a card or
  view invoices.

Billing is handled securely by our payment provider; DropTracker never stores
card details.
