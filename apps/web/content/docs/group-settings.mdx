# Configuring your group

Group owners and admins manage everything from the group's **Settings** tab
(`/groups/{id}/settings`).

## Channels

Pick the Discord channels for each notification type — drops, levels, personal
bests, combat achievements, pets, quests, and announcements. Leave a channel
unset to disable that notification.

## Drop notifications

- **Minimum value to notify** — suppress drops below a GP value.
- **Only send with images** — require a screenshot before posting.
- **Notify clogs / CAs / pets / quests** — toggle each category.

## Lootboards

Choose the board style and color behavior. You can also view a live, interactive
[lootboard](/docs/lootboards) on the group page.

## Seasonal settings

Most notification keys have a **seasonal** mirror, so you can run a parallel
seasonal configuration without losing your main one.

## Members

The **Members** tab lets you sync membership from Wise Old Man and hide/unhide
individual players from the group's leaderboards.
